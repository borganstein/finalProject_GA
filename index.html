Why Flashcards?

The scientific research suggests that the use of flashcards can dramatically improve memory retention as well as spontaneous recall. This makes it perfect for learning a new language. It hits those parts of the brain that are crucial in being able to learn a new language (or an old one!).**

It was a handy way of using simple javascript to showcase what I have learnt.

My German is a little bit rusty and I thought that using flashcards to help embed those German words in such a way that they can be easily recalled.

So please enjoy, have a bit of fun and let me know if I can make any improvements, you can find my contact details here.

** - Don't quote me on that but it sounds good!
